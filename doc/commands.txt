Regular commands:

   #save - save the character on the server
   #list_commands - lists the available server commands
   #follow character_name - you start following the selected character
   #stop_following - you stop following
   #release_followers - all followers stop following you
   #list_skills - lists your current skills with levels and experience

Test commands:

   #add_item itemID quantity - adds item with itemID to your inventory
   #change_health quantity - changes your health (negative or positive)
   #resurrect - brings character back from shadows dimension
